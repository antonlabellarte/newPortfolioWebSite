// Introduction

Graduated in Business Information Systems (SIA) at ITES Lenoci in Bari.
I completed a training course with the organization Informa Scarl to obtain a professional qualification as a web and software developer (EQF Level 4 of the European Qualifications Framework). My professional goals are focused on the IT sector, web development, and software development.
Before starting my career in software development, I gained professional experience as an Administrative Assistant in a wholesale tax warehouse for salts, tobacco products, and stationery items. In this role, I worked with SAP software, managed inventory reorganization, and supported logistics and goods delivery processes. This experience helped me develop a strong understanding of business workflows and operational efficiency. I later transitioned into software development, beginning my career as an AS/400 developer, where I built a solid foundation in enterprise systems and structured programming. Currently, I work in an IT consulting company as a mid-level low-code developer, primarily using the Microsoft Power Platform ecosystem, including Power Apps, Power BI, and SharePoint, to design and deliver business-oriented digital solutions. Alongside my low-code experience, I am increasingly focused on full-code development. I actively work with technologies such as Laravel, Angular, Java with Spring, and modern web languages, aiming to build scalable, maintainable, and high-quality applications while continuously growing as a developer.
